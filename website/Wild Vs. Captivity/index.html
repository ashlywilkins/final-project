Wild Vs. Captivity

It is very important for people to understand what killer whales are going through when they are taken from their home and placed in captivity. There are also whales born in captivity that have a very confused perspective on life due to never being able to life any of their life in the wild. The mammals that are kept in captivity are starting to behave poorly, attack traniers, and cause an upstir across the nation. People are seeming to not realize what we are actually doing to these poor creatures. It is time people understand how beautiful these aniumals are when they live in their own habitat. It is time to set them free.
