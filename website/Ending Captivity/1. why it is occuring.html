Why is Orca Captivity Occuring?

People all around the world are paying money to come see killer whales perform in extremely tiny tanks for their enjoyment. Ususally the orcas are happy mammals and interact well with their trainers but there have been many situations where this is not the case. Orca captivity is only still occuring because people are still paying to see them.
