Ending Captivity

It is time for Orca whale captivity to come to an end. What we are doing to our freindly comrads is inhumane and completely unfair to their species as a whole. There have been no attacks on humans by killer whales in the wild but there have been many cases of attacks in captivity. Captivity is unfair to these mammals who are restricted of their natural lifestyle for the entertainment of others. It is time we release these majestic creatures back into the wild, where they belong.
