What Makes A Whale A Killer Whale?

Killer whales are very different from most other types of whales. Technically, they are part of the dolphin family which makes them incredibly unique. Orca whales are extremely special due to many aspects of their lifestyle. To start, they look nothing like any other animal in the sea. They have very specific coloring patterns which sets them apart from all other sea mammals. They interact with their same species in a very friendly manner and spend much of their time playing with their family members. They hunt together and share their prize with the whole group. The Orca whale is an extremely unique mammal and we are so very lucky to have them in our world.
